Connected Defense

The complex threat situation as it is today, encourages various organizations to use various security products. If an attack is detected, targeted intelligence including malicious files, IP addresses, and C&C communications will be shared across products and environments (email, end-user, server, cloud, and network) for real-time protection. When these objects are found, they can be blocked automatically – one of the many benefits of Connected Defense.

All Trimitra solutions have security standards that have been certified by the Feasibility Center of the Ministry of Defense of the Republic of Indonesia, Trimitra provides defense-oriented network security solutions, command centers and intelligence solutions based on big data, artificial intelligence and tactical communication (C61SR). With several years of experience, our service has also developed various products based on internal R&D.

As part of our Connected Defense offering, full visibility across multiple technology layers allows you to quickly understand where the problem lies and how best to respond. This relates to the ability to respond quickly to encourage you to make real-time updates. A variety of security techniques to prevent future attacks, identify the root cause, and speed up repairs.
